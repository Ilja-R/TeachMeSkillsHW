Assignment:
1. Printing numbers in two goroutines
Task:
Create two goroutines:
• the first prints even numbers from 0 to 10,
• the second prints odd numbers from 1 to 9.
2. Parallel file loading
Task:
Simulate file downloads with different delays in goroutines:

• file2 takes 2 seconds,
